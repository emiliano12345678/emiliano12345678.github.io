Timeline of Basketball
1891 Dr james naismith invets basketball
1896 the first known professional basketball game is played in trenton, new jersey
1946 the basketball association of america (baa) is founded maurice podoloff is named leagues president
1947 the philadelphia warriors beat the chicago stages 4 games to 1, to win the first baa championship
1949 six teams from the nbl join the baa. the resulting 17 teams form the national basketball association (nba) and name maurice podloff as president
1950 the minneapolis lakers become the first team to win back to back championships
1961 the chicago packers enter the nba as the first expansion team
1966 the nba awards chicago with its third team, (the previous two had either moved, or disbanded)the bulls
1967 the nba gives teams to san diego(rockets), and the seattle(supersonics)
1979 the nba adopts a three point line
1980 the nba adimts the dallas mavericks bringing the nba to 23 teams
1996 the nba agrees to start a womens nation basketball association(wnba)
2008 the seattle supersonics relocate to oklahoma city and become the oklahoma city thunder, but as part of their agreement with the city for leaving the cannot keep the sonics history and thus are usually considered and expansion team
