Almond Green Salad

Fresh and Cripsy

Ingredients

1/2 pound bacon 2 tablespoons white vinegar 3 tablespoons honey 1/2 teaspoon dry hot mustard 1/2 teaspoon celery salt 1/2 teaspoon ground paprika 1/4 cup olive oil 1 head red leaf lettuce torn into bite-size pieces 1(15 ounce) can of mandarin oranges, drained 1 bunch green onions, diced 3/4 cup silvered almonds

Directions

1. In medium skillet over medium-heat, cook bacon until evenly brownd. Drain cool, and crumble. 2. To make the dressing, thoroughly blend the vinegar, honey, dry mustard, celery salt, paprika, and olive oil 3.Place letucce, oranges, green onions, bacon, and almonds in serving bowl. Toss with dressing and serve.
